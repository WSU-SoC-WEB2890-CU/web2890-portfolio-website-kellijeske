ABOUT ME

I’m Kelli Jeske, a beginner UX designer and front-end developer with plenty of graphic design and marketing experience. Through my studies and projects, I’ve developed skills in high-fidelity prototyping and crafting intuitive user flows that balance design and functionality.

I have done some real world web design projects, such as redesigning the website for Utah Contract Sewing, adding web content for local businesses in Logan, and designing a website for a friend’s custom sticker label business. I’m eager to grow by tackling more real-world projects. I’m passionate about simplifying complex processes and exploring how design can drive business success and improve user engagement.
